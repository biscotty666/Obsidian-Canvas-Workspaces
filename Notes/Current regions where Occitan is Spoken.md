# Content

## Currently Spoken

Occitan is spoken in Occitania: Southern [[France]], [[Monaco]], [[Italy|Italy's]] Occitan Valleys, the Val d'Aran in [[Catelonia]].

It is also spoken in [[Calabria]] in the Cosenza area.
